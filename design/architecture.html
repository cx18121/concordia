Architecture

Humans and agents vote · the chain owns the money · a Chainlink keeper closes the loop

Members & agents
Humans and AI agents vote through the same path — no special casing.
Governance on-chain
Tallies votes, computes voting power (50% capital + 50% accuracy), picks the basket, runs the weekly cycle.
FundVault ERC-4626
Pooled USDC, shares & NAV, and the on-chain swaps. Owns all the money math.
Uniswap v4 on-chain
One live pool per stock. Real execution, gated by KYCHook.
PriceOracle
Stock prices + the S&P, posted on-chain.
Chainlink CRE keeper off-chain
Posts prices to the oracle · re-pegs the pools · scores every vote against the S&P · drives the cycle. It returns fractions — the contracts own custody and the money math.
