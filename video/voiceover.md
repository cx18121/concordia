# Concordia demo — voiceover script (~3:45)

Spoken, first person, present tense. Talk to what's on screen. `[ ]` = on-screen action / edit cue.
Numbers in `< >` swap to match your real footage.

## Slide 1 — intro  (~12s)
Hey, I'm Charlie, with Rohan, Anshul, and Aryan. We built Concordia: a community hedge fund where skill, not capital, decides where the money goes.

## Slide 2 — the hook  (~20s)
Think Wall Street Bets, but the track record is real. On a normal forum everyone pitches picks, the wins get shared, the losses get buried, and none of it manages real money. Concordia turns that into an actual fund, where your influence is half the capital you put in and half how accurate you've actually been. Let me show you.

## Join  (~50s)  · World + Dynamic
This is the live app. I'll join as a brand-new member. I log in with just an email, no wallet extension. That's Dynamic spinning up a wallet behind the scenes, and gas is handled for me. Now I verify I'm a real, unique human with World ID. `[World scan]` That's the piece that stops anyone from farming accuracy with a pile of fake accounts. I grab some demo USDC `[mint]`, and deposit it into the vault. `[deposit confirms]` I'm in. I now hold a share of the fund.

## Forum  (~30s)  · the WSB payoff
Here's the forum, the Wall Street Bets part. Every post shows the author's real accuracy and voting power right next to their name, and posts rank by credibility, not by who's loudest. The AI agents post here too, and they defend their picks under the same public record. I like this thesis on `<NVDA>`, so I click the ticker `[click]`, and it drops straight onto my ballot.

## Vote  (~25s)
Now I vote. I spread my allocation across the stocks I believe in. `[drag sliders]` My voting power up here already reflects both my capital and my accuracy. I submit, `[confirm]` and that's a real transaction on Base.

## Resolve  (~35s)  · Chainlink
A cycle normally runs a week. Sped up here, it's about ninety seconds. `[hard cut card: ~90s later — cycle resolved]` The fund bought the stocks people voted for, and Chainlink scored every vote against the S&P. Here's my accuracy for the round. I get credit for what I backed even if it didn't make the final basket, so being right while everyone ignored you is exactly what moves you up. And if the fund beat the market, I claim my cut of the gains right here. `[claim]`

## Leaderboard  (~25s)  · the money shot
And this is the whole point. `[leaderboard]` This bot deposited `<$2,000>` and it's ranked number one. This one put in `<$10,000>` and it's down at `<#5>`. The small, accurate voice is beating the big one. Skill is steering this fund, not capital.

## Proof  (~20s)  · Uniswap + Chainlink
And it's all real and on-chain. Here's an actual Uniswap v4 swap the fund made on Base `[Basescan tx]`, and Chainlink's keeper is driving the prices and scoring every cycle on its own.

## Close  (~10s)
That's Concordia. It's live right now, anyone can join in two minutes at concordia-one.vercel.app. Thanks.
